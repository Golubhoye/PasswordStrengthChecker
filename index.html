Password Safety Tips

  • Use a long passphrase (≥12 chars) with mixed types.
  • Avoid personal info (names, dates) and common words.
  • Do not reuse passwords across sites.
  • Use a trusted password manager to store unique passwords.
  • Prefer passphrases (three or four random words) over single words.
